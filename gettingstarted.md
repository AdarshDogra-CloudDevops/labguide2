    ![](./media/selectregion.png)

**Note:** You can deploy the resources in any of the six regions mentioned below:

*  **us-east-1 (N. Virginia)**

*  **us-east-2 (Ohio)**

*  **us-west-2 (Oregon)**

*  **eu-central-1 (Frankfurt)**

*  **eu-west-2 (London)**

*  **ap-southeast-1 (Singapore)**
